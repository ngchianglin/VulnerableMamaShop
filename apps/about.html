Vulnerable Mama Shop

A vulnerable php application for learning SQL injection. "Mama shop" is a term that means small convenience store in Singapore. Such small stores are common during the 70s to 90s and mainly cater to residents living near-by. Gradually as Singapore develops and big business chain enter the market, Mama shops start to disappear.

This simple application is an online Mama Shop for practicing SQL injection. There is a vulnerability here. Can you find it ?
